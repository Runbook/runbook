What is CloudRoutes?

CloudRoutes is an application and server monitoring service that allows you to automate the resolution and mitigation of incidents. While it may sound complicated the gist of it is, we give you the tools to perform automated actions when your applications or servers are in trouble. Giving you the ability to reduce or even eliminating on call escalations.

Our system is made up of two parts, Monitors and Reactions. Monitors are used to check the status of your environment and Reactions are automated tasks that are called when monitors fail. We are working hard to give you the ability to automatically react to errors within your environment, without having to wake someone up.

Integrates with existing services

Using our system doesn't mean completely replacing your existing monitoring tools. We can integrate with existing monitoring tools like Datadog or New Relic. We also provide a simple to use API that can be used to integrate any custom monitoring tools.

Turn off your phone at night

Our Reactions can be used to change DNS via CloudFlare's API, send an email, or even Restart apache with SaltStack's salt-api. Our system lets you build automated responses that let you leave those late night alerts for the morning.

Open Source

CloudRoutes is released under the AGPL3 license and is created using the Assembly platform. If you have great ideas or feature requests you can join our team by opening a bounty or contributing code.
