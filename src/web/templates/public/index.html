Runbooks are as simple as one, two

Monitors

Monitors are used to check the status of your environment. They can be webhooks from Slack, they can check AWS instance status, they can be ping requests. Or, you can setup our TCP custom port to validate connectivity.

Reactions

Reactions are automated tasks that are called when Monitors fail. It can be anything from starting or restarting servers on AWS, Rackspace, DigitalOcean, or elsewhere, to running a custom script or changing DNS entries with CloudFlare. You know, all the first things you try when you get a 4am wake-up call.
